A Steampunk survival game set in a devastated world where you must explore to survive the harsh desert conditions of this steampunk driven world. In this project my responsibility was mainly around developing the AI in the game and extensive QA Testing and bugfixing. This project ended up winning the Gamer's Choice Award at the Swedish Game Awards 2025 and was also honorably nominated for Best Technology.
